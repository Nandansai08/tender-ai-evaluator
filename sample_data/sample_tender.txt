TENDER DOCUMENT
Tender Reference: CRPF/IGP/2024/CONSTRUCTION/001
Subject: Construction and Civil Works at CRPF Camp, New Delhi

1. INTRODUCTION

The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) invites sealed tenders from eligible contractors for
the construction of barracks, administrative block, and associated civil infrastructure at CRPF
Camp, Dwarka, New Delhi.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

The scope includes construction of:
(a) 2 Barracks blocks (each G+2 floors) — Total built-up area: 4,500 sq. mt.
(b) Administrative block (G+1) — 1,200 sq. mt.
(c) Internal roads, drainage, water supply and sanitation
(d) Electrical and fire-fighting installations
(e) Landscaping and boundary wall

Estimated cost: ₹12.5 Crore
Time of completion: 18 months from date of award

3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

3.1 Financial Eligibility

3.1.1 Minimum Annual Turnover:
The bidder must have had an average annual turnover of not less than ₹5 Crore (Rupees Five Crore)
from construction works during the last 3 consecutive financial years (2020-21, 2021-22, 2022-23).
Documentary evidence: Audited balance sheets or CA-certified turnover certificates for each year.

3.1.2 Net Worth:
The bidder must have a positive net worth as of the last completed financial year.
Documentary evidence: Audited balance sheet as on 31st March 2023.

3.2 Technical and Experience Eligibility

3.2.1 Similar Work Experience:
The bidder must have successfully completed at least 3 similar projects of civil/building
construction during the last 5 years (from 01.04.2019 to 31.03.2024), each with a contract
value of not less than ₹2 Crore.

"Similar work" means construction of residential/institutional/industrial buildings of G+1 or
above, including all civil, electrical, plumbing and sanitary works.

Documentary evidence: Completion certificates from competent government authority or
employer, clearly stating the name of work, contract amount, and date of completion.

3.2.2 Technical Manpower:
The bidder must have on its payroll or engage (on contractual basis) at minimum:
- 1 Graduate Civil Engineer with ≥5 years' experience
- 1 Diploma Civil Engineer with ≥3 years' experience
- 1 Electrical Engineer (Diploma or Degree)

3.2.3 Plant and Machinery:
The bidder must own or have committed access to the following minimum equipment:
- 1 Concrete Batching Plant
- 2 Transit Mixers
- 1 Tower Crane or equivalent hoisting equipment
- Bar Bending and Cutting Machines

3.3 Compliance / Regulatory Eligibility

3.3.1 GST Registration:
The bidder must possess a valid GST registration certificate (GSTIN) under the Goods and
Services Tax Act, 2017. The GSTIN number must be active and in good standing.

3.3.2 PAN Card:
The bidder must hold a valid Permanent Account Number (PAN) issued by the Income Tax
Department of India.

3.3.3 EPF Registration:
The bidder (if employing more than 20 persons) must have valid EPF registration under the
Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.

3.3.4 ESIC Registration:
The bidder (if employing more than 10 persons) must have valid ESIC registration under the
Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948.

3.4 Certification Requirements

3.4.1 ISO 9001 Certification:
Bidders must hold a valid ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification from a
NABCB-accredited certification body. The certificate must be valid on the date of bid
submission.

3.5 MSME Status (Optional)

MSME registered enterprises (Udyam Registration) are encouraged to participate and may be
eligible for certain concessions as per Government of India norms.

5. EVALUATION METHODOLOGY

Bids will be evaluated in two stages:
Stage 1: Technical Bid evaluation — Eligibility qualification as per Section 3
Stage 2: Financial Bid evaluation — L1 (lowest price) among technically qualified bidders

Only bidders who qualify Stage 1 will have their financial bids opened.
